Our Mission

My Skin Dermatology's mission is to provide the best possible care for our patients, at all stages of the treatment process. At My Skin Dermatology our goal is to provide a patient-friendly environment for a community of all ages, races and genders. Dr. Kechichian's successful practice in Palmdale led him to expand into Santa Clarita to serve the local community. Dr. Kechichian is committed to improving the health of the community by donating a sum of its proceeds yearly to the American Cancer Society. His primary goal is and has always been to treat people and not just symptoms to ensure their ultimate health. We offer a comprehensive range of medical, surgical, and cosmetic dermatology in an elegant, custom office designed by Dr. Kechichian himself.

Services At A Glance

At My Skin Dermatology, we diagnose and treat medical dermatology, benign and malignant disorders of the skin, scalp conditions and hair loss, and nail disorders. We also offer aesthetic dermatology for enhancing your natural features, and we treat skin changes associated with aging. We offer a full range of services including but not limited to:

  • Botox
